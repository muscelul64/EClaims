# Porsche E-Claims Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 31, 2025**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Porsche E-Claims ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- Name and contact information
- Driver's license information
- Insurance policy details
- Vehicle registration information

### Device Information
- **Camera Access**: We access your device camera to capture photos of vehicle damage, accident scenes, and insurance documents required for claims processing.
- **Location Data**: We collect location information to accurately record incident locations for insurance claims.
- **Audio Recording**: May be used for voice notes during claim documentation.

### Storage Access
- We access device storage to save and retrieve claim-related photos and documents.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Process insurance claims efficiently
- Document vehicle damage and accidents
- Provide accurate location data for incident reports
- Store and organize claim-related documentation
- Improve our services and user experience

## Camera Permission Usage

Our app requires camera access to:
- **Capture Damage Photos**: Take pictures of vehicle damage for insurance documentation
- **Document Incidents**: Photograph accident scenes, road conditions, and other relevant evidence
- **Insurance Documents**: Scan insurance cards, driver's licenses, and vehicle registration
- **Statement Documentation**: Visual evidence to support claim statements

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is encrypted and stored securely
- Photos and documents are processed locally on your device
- Sensitive information is transmitted using industry-standard encryption
- We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information

## Location Data Usage

Location information is used to:
- Record accurate incident locations
- Provide context for insurance claims
- Assist with emergency services if needed
- Improve routing and location-based features

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except:
- To insurance providers for legitimate claim processing
- To legal authorities when required by law
- To emergency services when necessary for safety

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your data
- Withdraw consent for data collection
- Disable camera or location access (though this may limit functionality)

## Third-Party Services

Our app may integrate with:
- Insurance provider systems
- Emergency services
- Cloud storage services for backup
- Analytics services for app improvement

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of any material changes through the app or other appropriate means.
